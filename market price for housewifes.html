Problem Statement

The current mandi chain isn't working for farmers

Most crops still move through several intermediaries before reaching a buyer — and value leaks out at every stop.

  • Farmers often receive low prices for their produce.
  • Middlemen take a significant share of the profit.
  • Buyers end up paying higher prices than they should.
  • Farmers struggle to find reliable, repeat buyers.
  • Price information is not transparent across the chain.

Core Features

What the platform actually does

Built around the five problems above — each feature closes one gap in the old system.

01

Live crop listings

Farmers post crop type, quantity, quality grade, and asking price directly — no agent required.

02

Transparent price board

Real-time and historical prices by crop and region, so both sides know what's fair before they trade.

03

Direct messaging & offers

Buyers and farmers negotiate and confirm orders in-app, cutting out the back-and-forth through agents.

04

Verified ratings

Both farmers and buyers build a trust score from past trades, so reliability replaces guesswork.

05

Logistics matching

Connects confirmed orders with local transport options, so produce still reaches the buyer efficiently.

06

Secure payments

Escrow-style payment release on delivery confirmation, protecting both farmer and buyer.
